Shipping and Returns

Shipping

Order processing will begin 24 hours after moment of purchase. Your order ship within 2-3 business days from the beginning or "processing" status. Shipping rates and times may vary based on distance and weather conditions.


Return Policy

In the event of damaged product, we will replace the full order at no cost to you. You may return any unused and unopened concentrates within an order so long as it is within 30 days of purchase, free of shipping charge.

Privacy Policy

Penny Juice reserves the right to maintain a record of your personal information, such as address, contact information, order history, and other associated user account, ordering or billing information. Your personal data is not shared nor sold to third party affiliations. All user data is deleted once accounts are deleted.
